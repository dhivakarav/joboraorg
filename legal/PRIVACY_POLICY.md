## 1. Who we are
Jobara ("we", "us") helps job seekers discover matching jobs, optimize
applications, and apply to and track jobs across supported platforms.

## 2. Data we collect
- **Account:** name, email, phone, password (stored hashed).
- **Resume & profile:** your uploaded resume (PDF) and parsed details (skills,
  experience, education, location, LinkedIn/portfolio links). Resumes are
  **encrypted at rest**.
- **Application data:** jobs you view, applications you submit, their status, and
  **submission evidence** (confirmation page screenshots/URLs/IDs) you generate.
- **Screening answers** you provide for applications.
- **Payment data:** processed by **Razorpay**; we store subscription status, not
  full card details.
- **Usage/analytics:** device, pages, events (via [PostHog]) — subject to consent.
- **Communications:** support messages, feedback.

## 3. How we use it
- Provide the service: parse your resume, match jobs, and — **only when you
  explicitly approve** — submit your application and store proof of submission.
- Send transactional + (with consent) product/marketing emails.
- Improve the product (aggregated/anonymized analytics).
- Billing, fraud prevention, security, and legal compliance.
- **We never fabricate applications or submit on your behalf without your
  explicit, per-application approval.**

## 4. Legal basis / consent
We process your data to perform our contract with you and with your consent
(DPDP: consent + legitimate uses). You may withdraw consent at any time
(see §8).

## 5. Sharing & disclosure
We share data only as needed to operate:
- **Job platforms** (e.g., Greenhouse) — to submit applications **you approve**,
  using **your** information.
- **Razorpay** — payment processing.
- **Email/analytics providers** (e.g., [Resend/SES], [PostHog]) under data
  processing agreements.
- **Legal** — if required by law.
We do **not** sell your personal data.
